Recap, week of 11/19/17



19 November, 2017


What a weekend.

Thursday night was our unveiling of the new Underground Music Night, which went pretty well. Northgate was pretty dead because everyone had midterms on Friday, so I'm willing to chalk it up as a good pilot run.

Friday night was Eric's birthday, and it was absolutely nuts. We ended up at J's Rooftop Ruckus gig at the Corner in time to see Wav-E play. EDM is not dead in College Station, and that set was proof. I threw together a little video from one of my snapchats here.



Underground Music Nights



16 November, 2017


If you've been around here for a minute, you might remember DJs of Aggieland's old Underground Music Nights. Actually, those were my first couple of shows as a DJ. In the old days, we had a few regular tables outnumbered by angry patrons wanting to hear normal Northgate music, which we begrudgingly provided. But in the last year or so, UMN has gained a lot of traction with the College Station EDM scene and we've got a pretty strong following.

Since taking over as president of DJOA, I haven't played on many of our "club gigs" because I wanted to showcase our members from top to bottom. However, my boy Wav-E is in town tonight and it's been like a year since we've played a show together. So here I am, gig-prepping and getting excited about getting to play whatever I want once again. See everyone tonight!
